About Us



We've been making healthy cuisines for the past 5 years


Hello Everyone,   Welcome to our restaurant!

We've been proudly serving delicious and healthy cuisines to our valued customers for the past 5 years. Our journey began in 2019, and since then, we've been dedicated to providing exceptional dining experiences. Located in the beautiful Place of Dharan, Nepal, our restaurant offers a unique blend of flavors and a warm, inviting atmosphere. Whether you're craving traditional Nepali dishes or looking to try something new, our menu has something for everyone.
